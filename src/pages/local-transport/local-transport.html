Local Transport
By Air By Train By Bus
Nearest Airport for reaching Sri Shakambhari Mata Mandir is Jaipur. Devotees can hire cab or take a direct bus for Sakarai where the temple is located. It is around 150 km from Jaipur .
Nearest Railway Station is Jhunjhunu / Sikaar. Devotees can take train either upto Jhunjhunu or Jaipur, from one can reach either by bus or car.There are several direct buses available from Jaipur to Sakarai.
